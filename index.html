About

I am an associate professor in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA).

I got my Ph.D degree in Dec. 2019 from School of Software of Nanjing University, China, supervised by Prof. Baowen Xu, and Prof. Zhenyu Chen. I received both my master degree under the supervision of Lecturer Yan Hu and Prof. He Jiang, and bachelor degree from the School of Software of Dalian University of Technology in 2013 and 2010, respectively. From Sept. 2017 to Sept. 2018, I was a visiting student of Prof. David Lo in Singapore Management Universiy.

My research focuses on mining softare repositories (e.g., bug reports, GitHub data) to uncover interesting and actionable information to help improve software quality and developer productivity. I have employed and developed techniques including natural language processing, machine learning, and program analysis to transform passive software engineering data into new insights and automated tools like bug triaging and bug localization tools.
